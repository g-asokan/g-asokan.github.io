Police Budget and Overtime Data report

A data report on Boston Police department's budget and use of overtime.

The project was started in collaboration with the ACLU Massachusetts, Progressive Massachusetts, and City Councilor Ricardo Arroyo's office to examine the use of overtime by the Boston Police department.

Impact

The project's data report was used in the Boston City council's police department budget hearing, you can find the full project's visualizations here.
